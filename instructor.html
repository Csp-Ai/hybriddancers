Meet Pearla

Pearla launched Hybrid Dancers after years of training in hip-hop, shuffle, and contemporary dance. She refined her technique in ASU’s dance program while performing at festivals like EDC and Coachella.

She teaches with a growth mindset—believing anyone can elevate their movement through consistent practice and curiosity. Classes combine shuffle foundations with lyrical control so you learn to adapt to any rhythm.

When crafting choreography, Pearla mixes street energy with structured drills to challenge you while keeping the vibe positive and supportive.

Credentials: ASU Dance graduate • 8+ years festival performer • 500+ students taught
